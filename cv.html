Tokyo Institute of Technology

Researcher, Dept. of Communications and Computer Engineering

Apr. 2014-Till Date

Work on two different research direction: (1) design and implementation of energy efficient and low cost embedded processor. The processor is described in C language, which cab be synthesized by any commercial or open-source HLS tools. I explore efficient C coding style to produce small and energy efficient circuits. In addition to that different architectural techniques are also taken into for smaller and low power design; (2) investigating efficient hardware and software techniques to detect and recover both permanent and timing faults. These techniques covers a wide range of processor architectrues such as small scale embedded processor, high-end commercial processor, and reconfigurable processor.
