Tanvir Ahmed

Researcher @ Fujitsu Laboratories Ltd
tanvira@ieee.org

I will join the Computer System Laboratory at Fujitsu Laboratories Ltd. in Sept. 2016, and currently I am a Visiting Researcher at Hara Laboratory in Tokyo Institute of Technology. I was briefly working as a Platform Acceleration Architect at SBI BITS to develop a FPGA based trading system, and before that I was a full time Researcher at Prof. Yuko Hara-Azumi's Lab at Tokyo Institute of Technology.

I graduated from Nara Institute of Science and Technology in March 2014. Before that I was at Linkpoing University for M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering. Here is my CV.

My research interests lie in the broad areas of computer architecture and embedded system, with particular focus on the development of accelerator and small-scale processor for next generation application, with the consideration of reliability, power, and computation efficiency. The various topics I am interested in include (but not limited to) fault tolerance, power and performance aware accelerator, embedded system design, worst-case delay optimization in circuit, architecture, and software level, as well as scheduling and resource management.
