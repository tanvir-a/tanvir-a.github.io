Tanvir Ahmed

Senior Research Scientist @ EdgeCortix, Inc.
tanvira@edgecortix.com, tanvira@ieee.org

I am working as a Senior Research Scientist at EdgeCortix, Inc. since Aug. 2021. At EdgeCortix, I am exploring highly reconfigurable hardware architecture and related technologies for machine learning. Prior to joining EdgeCortix, I have spent several years in Preferred Networks, Inc, and Fujitsu Laboratories Ltd. I also spent two years as a Postdoctoral researcher at Tokyo Institute of Technology. Most of my previous experiences and current works are solving different aspects of processor or accelerator architecture, and compiler technologies.

I graduated from Nara Institute of Science and Technology in March 2014. Before that, I got an M.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from Linkoping University. Details about my educational background and employment history can be found here.

My research interests lie in the broad areas of computer architecture and embedded systems, with a particular focus on the development of accelerators, and small-scale processors for next-generation applications, with the consideration of reliability, power, and computation efficiency. The various topics I am interested in include (but not limited to) power and performance-aware accelerators and processor architecture for machine learning, approximate computing in the circuit, architecture, and software level.
