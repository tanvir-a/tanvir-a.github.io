Biography

I am a researcher in the School of Engineering at Tokyo Institute of Technology and a memory of Hara laboratory. My research interests are in the broad aspects of computer systems, focusing on high performance, energy efficiency, and reliability of general-purpose computer architectures, domain-specific accelerators, and highly reconfigurable architectures. Recently, I became interested in machine learning for electronic design automation (EDA) optimization.

Before joining TokyoTech, I spent several years in industries (Fujitsu laboratories ltd, Preferred Networks, Inc., and Edgecortix, Inc.), where I was involved in designing and developing machine learning and neuromorphic accelerators.
